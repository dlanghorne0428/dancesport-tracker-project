DanceSport Tracker Ranking System

Ranking System


Ranking Groups

  • There is a separate ranking list for each combination of couple type and dance style (Smooth, Rhythm, Standard, Latin, Cabaret, etc.)
  • There are five types of couples:
    • Professional Couples
    • Pro-Am Couples - adult student with a pro instructor
    • Amateur Couples - two adult amateurs
    • Junior Pro-Am Couples - junior student with a pro instructor
    • Junior Amateur Couples- two junior amateurs

Earning Points

  • Couples earn points based on their performance at NDCA competitions.
  • All Multi-dance heats earn points.
  • Professional Cabaret / Showdance / Theater Arts events earn points.
  • Single-dance freestyle heats do not earn points. These results are not stored in DanceSport Tracker.
  • Points are calculated based on three factors
    1. Heat Value
    2. Number of Preliminary Rounds
    3. Couple Placement or Number of Recall Votes

Preliminary Rounds

For each preliminary round danced, the Heat Value is increased by 10.

    Some Examples
  • An Open Professional event with a quarterfinal has a value of 40 (20 + 10 for semis + 10 for quarters)
  • An Pro-Am Silver Scholarship event with a semifinal has a value of 30 (15 + 5 for scholarship + 10 for semis)
  • An Amateur Pre-Champ event with no preliminary rounds has a value of 15
